Frequently asked questions

Is the Basic plan really free?

Yes. You can create and manage a standard listing at no cost, forever. Upgrade only if you want more visibility and features.

Who can list on Go Local Siouxland?

Any business serving the tri-state area — Sioux City, South Sioux City, North Sioux City, Dakota Dunes and the surrounding communities.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Absolutely. Plans are month-to-month with no contracts — upgrade, downgrade or cancel anytime from your dashboard.

How do featured placements work?

Featured and Premium listings appear above standard results within their category and in rotating homepage spotlights.
